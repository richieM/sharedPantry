    Smart Bin

    Measures amount of ingredients stored. Three inventory status LED indicators.
    next prototype
    This is our first smart bin prototype. The weight sensor system was build into the bin. Three LED lights indicate whether your ingredient was sufficiently stocked, out of stock, or was currently being delivered. After taking this prototype out into the field, we learned that there were a few features that needed improvements.
    1) The bin needed to be washable
    2) The system had to support bins of multiple sizes
    3) The indicator should provide a warning of when an ingredient is almost out rather than a binary “out” or “not out”.
